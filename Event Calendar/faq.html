1) What is Event Calendar?

ans)Event Calendar is a website which helps you view or post events.


2)How to post an event?

ans)You need to first login through your account and then select "Post Event" option available on the webpage.


3) How can I change my contact number?

ans)You cannot change your contact number which is your login id. If there is a change in your number, than you have to update it in your 'Other Contact' on your profile.


4) What if I come across some kind of error which shouldn't be happening?

ans)If you are getting an error that you are unable to understand, send a mail to info@eventcalendar.com with the title as "Error" along with the contact detail and the error you are facing.

5) What is the age limit to join Event Calendar?

ans)There is no age limit to join Event Calendar, but there are certain events in which age is an eligibility criteria.
