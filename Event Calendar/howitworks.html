step 1:Registration


To view or post events on our website, you have to first "Register" and provide us with little details about yourself with your contact number that we will need to contact you. To proceed with the further operations on the webpage,you need to login through your account.


step 2:View Events


After logging in,you can simply view the events from the list of events in the "All Events" Page.


step 3:Post Events


If you wish to post any event,you can continue with that only after agreeing to the given terms and conditions.
